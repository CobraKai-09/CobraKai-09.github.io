About!

I'm a sophomore at Crimson Cliffs High School.

I'm auditioning for the jazz band, and I worked really hard to get ready for it. Every day, I practiced my instrument, focused on getting better, and tried to learn more about jazz music. I asked for help when I needed it and kept going even when it was tough. I always try to give my best effort, and this audition was no different. No matter what happens, I’m proud of how hard I worked and how much I’ve improved. I am also working on maintaining a 4.0 GPA throughout my whole high school journey.


My karate journey has been a path of discipline, growth, and perseverance. I started as a beginner with little knowledge of the art, but over time, through consistent training and dedication, I developed both my skills and confidence. Each belt advancement marked not just a physical achievement, but a deeper understanding of focus, respect, and inner strength. I’ve faced challenges—physically demanding practices, mental hurdles, and even moments of self-doubt—but overcoming them has made me stronger both on and off the mat. Karate has become more than just a sport for me; it's a lifelong discipline that continues to shape who I am.
